The Four Tones
1st High flat mā 妈 mother
2nd Rising má 麻 hemp
3rd Dip & rise mǎ 马 horse
4th Falling mà 骂 scold
0th Neutral / light ma 吗 (question)
Tone Sandhi Rules
3rd + 3rd → 2nd + 3rd
你好 nǐ hǎo → ní hǎo
一 yī before 4th → yí
一个 yí gè
一 yī before 1st/2nd/3rd → yì
一只 yì zhī
Reading Strategy
Look at the tone mark on the vowel — it draws the pitch shape.
ā flat line · á going up · ǎ dip down · à going down
No mark = light/neutral tone (short, unstressed).
